## 🔑 Key Findings

- 💰 Total Revenue: $2,297,200.86
- 📈 Total Profit: $286,397.02
- 📊 Profit Margin: 12.47%
- 🏆 Best Category: Technology
- 🌍 Best Region: West
- ⚠️ High discounts above 20% cause profit losses

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Reduce discounts above 20% immediately
2. Focus marketing budget on West region
3. Expand Technology product line
4. Target Consumer segment for growth
5. Improve shipping speed to reduce Days to Ship
